Amy Haigh

info@amyhaigh.com

Freelance Graphic Designer with 10+ years creative experience, based in SE London.

I specialise in environmental and exhibition graphics, interpretation design, and image work within the cultural sector—with particular emphasis on translating graphic elements for use across print and spacial interfaces.

I hold an MA in Information Experience Design from the Royal College of Art, a research-based course exploring the possibilities of experiencing information within digital and physical spaces. I take my work very seriously and am meticulous in my practices, resulting in consistently positive feedback from clients and end users.

Full portfolio available on request.
